Egg Toast

Eggcellent!

Toast with protein on top

Ingredients

  • 1 piece of toast
  • 1 egg
  • A pinch of Salt
  • A pinch of Pepper

Steps

  1. Make toast from this recipe
  2. If not already hot from making toast, heat pan to medium
  3. Add cracked egg onto the pan
  4. Add Salt and Pepper
  5. Cook until your preferred texture
  6. Serve on top of toast
