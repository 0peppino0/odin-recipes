Toast

Well done sliced bread

Healthy, Yummy, Colorful

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 slice of bread

Steps

  1. Heat pan to medium
  2. Butter both sides of the sliced bread
  3. Put slice of bread on pan
  4. Cook 10 min both sides or until chocolatey brown
  5. Serve with 2 glasses of water (it's kinda dry)
