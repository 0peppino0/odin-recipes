BLToast

Enough greens to say you eat your veggies

Bacon, Lettuce, Toast (I couldn't afford tomatoes)

Ingredients

  • 2 strips of bacon
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 2 slices of toast

Steps

  1. Heat pan to medium
  2. Seperate and wash lettuce leaf for later
  3. When the pan is hot, cook bacon until crispy
  4. Set bacon aside for later use
  5. Make toast(use bacon fat for bonus flavour)
  6. Assemble all items in this order
    • Toast
    • Bacon
    • Lettuce
    • Toast
  7. Put on plate
  8. Eat
